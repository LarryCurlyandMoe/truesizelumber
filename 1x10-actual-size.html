What size is a 1×10, really?

A 1×10 is sold and labeled as 1×10, but it actually measures ¾" × 9¼". The difference comes from milling and drying — the board starts closer to its named size, then shrinks as it's surfaced smooth and dried to standard moisture content.

A 1×10 is actually ¾" × 9¼" — not 1×10. Common uses include framing, structural support, and general construction.
